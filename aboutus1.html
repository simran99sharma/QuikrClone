About Us




We are an online buying and selling website. The aim is to make the process of buying and selling easy and hassle-free, specially in todays time where everyone is working and no one has the time to ship off an old item that they no longer use.

The transaction aims to go smoothly with the help of middle men hired by us to pack and ship sale items to warehouses and then to buyers once payment is recieved. The entire process aims to be transparent with verification of buyers and sellers happening in person by our company.
